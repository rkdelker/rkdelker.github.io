Rebecca Delker is a Molecular and Cell Biologist currently conducting research at Columbia University and teaching at Manhattan College. She received her BS in Biochemistry from UC San Diego and her PhD in Molecular Immunology from Rockefeller University. Throughout the course of her scientific career, she realized the critical importance of looking at Science through the lens of philosophy and ethics to maximize its benefits and minimize its harms. Rebecca infuses these ideas into her research and teaching, and explores them in more depth in her writing.

She does her best thinking in the wilderness.
